Senior Interior Designer

New York

About the Role

We're seeking an experienced Senior Interior Designer to lead residential and commercial projects from concept to completion. You'll work directly with clients, manage junior designers, and help shape our design direction.

Responsibilities

  • Develop creative design concepts that meet client objectives
  • Create detailed drawings, specifications, and mood boards
  • Manage project timelines and budgets
  • Source materials and furnishings
  • Mentor junior design staff

Requirements

  • 7+ years of interior design experience
  • Bachelor's degree in Interior Design or related field
  • Proficiency in AutoCAD, SketchUp, and Adobe Creative Suite
  • Strong portfolio of completed projects
  • Excellent communication and client management skills

Chicago

Remote

Requirements
